Grilled Cheese

Description:

This grilled cheese. It's self explanatory. It's good. People like it. It's a cheese sandwhich heated up with butter. All good things.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Butter the pan
  2. lay bread down
  3. stack cheese
  4. put bread on top
  5. flip when crispy
  6. Plate when other side is done
